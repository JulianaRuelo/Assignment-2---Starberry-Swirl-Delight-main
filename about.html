(About)

Starberry Swirl Delight is a typeface by Little Starseed.

It was created on July 20, 1998. It is a very whimsical display font featuring strawberries inside some of the characters. Most of the letters have swirls on the ends. The line variation inside each character gives the impression of syrup or perhaps icing. Starberry Swirl Delight brings the words “fun” and “sweet” to mind.
